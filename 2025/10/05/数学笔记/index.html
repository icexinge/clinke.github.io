1、矩阵的迹的性质
  • 线性算子

tr(A+B)=tr(A)+tr(B),tr(cA)=ctr(A)tr(A+B)=tr(A)+tr(B), tr(cA)=ctr(A) tr(A+B)=tr(A)+tr(B),tr(cA)=ctr(A)

  • 转置不变性

tr(A)=tr(AT)tr(A)=tr(A^{T}) tr(A)=tr(AT)

  • 共轭转置

tr(AH)=tr(A)‾tr(A^{H})=\overline{tr(A)} tr(AH)=tr(A)​

  • 循环性质

tr(AB)=tr(BA)tr(ABC)=tr(CAB)=tr(BCA)tr(AB)=tr(BA) \\ tr(ABC)=tr(CAB)=tr(BCA) tr(AB)=tr(BA)tr(ABC)=tr(CAB)=tr(BCA)

  • 项式不变性

B=P−1AP⇒tr(A)=tr(B)B=P^{-1}AP \Rightarrow tr(A)=tr(B) B=P−1AP⇒tr(A)=tr(B)

  • 特征值之和等于迹

tr(A)=∑i=1nλitr(A)=\sum_{i=1}^{n}\lambda_{i} tr(A)=i=1∑n​λi​

  • 迹与 Frobenius 范数

∥A∥F2=∑i=1m∑j=1n∣aij∣2=tr(AHA)=tr(AAH)\|\mathbf{A}\|_{F}^{2}=\sum\limits_{i=1}^{m}\sum\limits_{j=1}^{n}|a_{ij}|^{2}=\mathrm{tr}(\mathbf{A}^{H}\mathbf{A})=\mathrm{tr}(\mathbf{A}\mathbf{A}^{H}) ∥A∥F2​=i=1∑m​j=1∑n​∣aij​∣2=tr(AHA)=tr(AAH)

  • 迹与行列式

det(eA)=etr(A)det(e^{A})=e^{tr(A)} det(eA)=etr(A)

  • 迹与导数

∂tr(AB)∂A=BT\frac{\partial{ {tr}(AB)} }{\partial{A}}=B^{T} ∂A∂tr(AB)​=BT

2、在复数域上使用施密特正交化时的内积与实数域不同
  • 实数域

⟨x,y⟩≜xTy=∑i=1nxiyi\langle x,y\rangle \triangleq x^{\mathrm{T}}y=\sum_{i=1}^{n}x_{i}y_{i} ⟨x,y⟩≜xTy=i=1∑n​xi​yi​

  • 复数域

⟨x,y⟩≜xTy=∑i=1nxiyi‾\langle x,y\rangle \triangleq x^{\mathrm{T}}y=\sum_{i=1}^{n}x_{i}\overline{y_{i}} ⟨x,y⟩≜xTy=i=1∑n​xi​yi​​

∣ab∣≤∣a∣∣b∣\left| ab \right| \le \left| a \right|\left| b \right| ∣ab∣≤∣a∣∣b∣

  • 积分形式

(∫abf(x)g(x)dx)2=(∫abf(x)2dx)(∫abg(x)2dx)\left( \int_a^b f(x)g(x)dx\right)^2 = \left( \int_a^b f(x)^2dx \right)\left( \int_a^b g(x)^2dx \right) (∫ab​f(x)g(x)dx)2=(∫ab​f(x)2dx)(∫ab​g(x)2dx)
